Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Ground Beef
  • Salsa
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Brown the meat.
  2. Prepare the tortilla chips on a baking sheet
  3. Top the chips with cheese and meat.
  4. Place in the oven for 20 minutes.
  5. Once baked, add additional toppings.
